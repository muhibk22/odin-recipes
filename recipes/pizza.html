Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe features a crispy crust, flavorful tomato sauce, and a blend of melted cheeses, perfect for a fun family dinner or a casual gathering with friends. Customize with your favorite toppings for a unique and delicious creation.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Place a pizza stone or an inverted baking sheet in the oven to heat.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness. Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper.
  3. Brush the dough with olive oil, then spread the pizza sauce evenly over the surface, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheeses over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings, distributing them evenly over the pizza.
  6. Carefully transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper) onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
